The Importance of Understanding the Rules

  • Set Up: Players take turns hitting a ball (or similar object) over a net or designated area.
  • Scoring: Points are scored when the opposing team fails to return the ball or misses a target.
  • Game Duration: Matches typically end when a pre-determined score is reached, or a specific time limit has passed.
